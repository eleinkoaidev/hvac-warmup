Common Error Codes

01

Ignition Failure

System failed to detect flame. Check: Gas supply, flame sensor (clean/replace), igniter resistance.

02

Pressure Switch Open

Draft inducer problem. Check: Blocked flue, inducer motor amp draw, cracked hose to pressure switch.

03

Limit Switch Open

System overheating. Check: Dirty filter, blocked returns, blower motor capacitor/failure.

04

Rollout Switch Open

Flame rollout detected. Critical Safety Issue. Check: Heat exchanger for cracks, proper draft.

Safety Tips

  • LOTO (Lockout / Tagout)

    ALWAYS disconnect power and lock the disconnect before servicing any moving parts or electrical connections. Verify dead with a meter.

  • Capacitor Safety

    Capacitors hold a charge even after power is off. Short the terminals with a screwdriver (using the insulated handle) before touching.

  • PPE Checklist

    Gloves for sheet metal. Safety glasses for refrigerant/brazing. Non-slip shoes for attics/ladders.
